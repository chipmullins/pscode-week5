Pseudo Code for making a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich.

1. Open pantry
2. Grab Peanut Butter
3. Grab Jelly
4. Grab Bread
5. Grab butterknife
6. Open peanut butter
7. Spread peanut butter onto one slice of bread
8. Spread jelly onto other side of bread
9. Put the two pieces of bread together
10. Put away items
